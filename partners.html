TapIn Mobile Solutions

The GDL has partnered with TapIn Mobile Solutions to run its events based programs on the world's premier sports technology platform. The TapIn platform runs on all mobile devices using IOS and Android systems. Through this partnership, TMS has customized its solutions to enable GDL programs to deliver FUN, targeted development focused content and functionality incliuding:

Palla Sportswear

In addition to the TapIn Mobile Solutions partnership, Grassroots Development League has partnered with Palla Sportswear to bring the game to deserving children. As the exclusive sportswear brand of the GDL, Palla, will donate products to players and teams playing in TapIn league around the world each time a GDL player, parent, referee, or coach buys Palla products. By shopping at Pallasport.com, every item you purchase will result in a similar item being donated to a child or team playing in a TapIn developing nation league. Buy a ball they give a ball. Buy a pair of shin gaurds, they give a pair. It's that simple.
